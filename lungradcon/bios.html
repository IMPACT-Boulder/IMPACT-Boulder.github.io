  • Alessondra Springmann, University of Arizona

    Alessondra Springmann is a third-year graduate student in the Lunar & Planetary Laboratory at the University of Arizona studying asteroids, comets, and meteorites with Dante S. Lauretta and Walter M. Harris. She spent two years working on the thermal properties of primitive meteorites and their relevance to preserving samples returned from asteroids, and currently observes three Jupiter-family comets passing close by Earth, the last time comets will be this well positioned for observing until 2038.
