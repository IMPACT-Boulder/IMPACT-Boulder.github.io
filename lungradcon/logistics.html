LunGradCon: Logistics

LunGradCon

Lungradcon 2024 will be held in-person on July 17th in Salon G of the Hotel at the University of Maryland, located at 7777 Baltimore Avenue, College Park, Maryland.

Light food and refreshments will be served at the beginning of the day. After each speaker is done, the session chairs will open the floor to questions from the audience about any of the topics in the session, and general discussion will be encouraged.

Group Social Dinner

The dinner will be on Sunday night at 6:30pm. The dinner will be at Blaze pizza (7419 Baltimore Ave, College Park, MD 20740), which is about an 8 minute walk from the conference hotel. They have options for a variety of dietary restrictions, including gluten-free, dairy-free, vegetarian, and vegan. We will have an optional meet up at 6:15pm in the conference hotel lobby for anyone that wants to walk over to Blaze together.
