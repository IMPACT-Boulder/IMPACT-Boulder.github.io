LunGradCon: Logistics

LunGradCon

The 17th annual Lunar and Small Bodies Graduate Forum (LunGradCon 2026) will be held in-person on July 20, 2026 at NASA Ames Research Center. The conference will take place from 9 AM to 4 PM in Building N17, room 202 on the second floor. Snacks and refreshments will be provided, but not lunch.
