Recent news for IMPACT

03/19/2020: Leiden University Professor Jordy Bouwman gives talk at LASP/IMPACT.

Jordy Bouwman, an Assistant Professor at the Leiden Observatory, a part of Leiden University, gave a talk at LASP as part of the Cosmoschemistry professorship search at LASP and IMPACT. His research follows the evolution of interstellar dust grains and meteorites. A video of his talk can be found on the IMPACT vimeo account here.

3/12/2020: IMPACT student participates in American Geophysical Union press conference

Washington University in St. Louis Research Assistant Professor Nan Liu gave a talk at LASP as part of the Cosmoschemistry professorship search at LASP and IMPACT. Her research uses isotopic ratios to study the history, distribution, and origins of interstellar dust grains. You can watch the video of her talk here.

03/05/2020: NRAO Researcher Brett McGuire gives talk at LASP/IMPACT.

Brett McGuire, a Hubble Fellow at the National Radio Astronomy Observatory, gave a talk at LASP as part of the IMPACT faculty search. His talk covered his research into the molecular makeup of the galaxy using rotational spectroscopy. A video of his talk can be found on the IMPACT vimeo account here.

12/15/2019: IMPACT student participates in American Geophysical Union press conference

IMPACT student Lihsia Yeo, who works with Xu Wang on laboratory simulations of the solar wind and its interaction with the moon, participated in a Geoscience Grab Bag press conference held at the 2019 American Geophysical Union (AGU).

You can view the press conference here.
